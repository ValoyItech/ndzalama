Meet Mr Amazing

Meet Mr Amazing

Ishmael Ndzalama Baloyi

Founder & Creative Director of Distinguished Holdings

Popularly known as "Mr Amazing" or "Mr A", Ishmael has been passionate about style since his early school days, where his impeccable dress sense set him apart from his peers. After 10 years of dedicated service in the South African Police Services, he followed his true calling back to fashion.

As a style saviour to South Africans, Mr A has dressed over 100 public figures and celebrated icons, becoming a force to be reckoned with in the fashion industry. His expertise spans styling, tailoring, and fashion blogging.

Through the Distinguished Skills Development Centre, he educates and empowers upcoming talent in style etiquette, fabric selection, and conceptualizing ideas that match clients' needs. Whether you need style redefinition, occasional couture advice, or fabric selection guidance - Mr Amazing is your guy.
