About Distinguished Wines

Distinguished Wines is a boutique winery specializing in crafting exceptional red blends that showcase the art of blending. Our passion is to create complex, full-bodied wines that delight the senses and leave a lasting impression.

Our Philosophy

We believe that blending different grape varieties allows us to create wines that are greater than the sum of their parts. Our winemakers carefully select and blend the finest grapes to craft wines that are balanced, nuanced, and refined.

Each bottle represents our commitment to excellence and our deep respect for the winemaking tradition that has been passed down through generations in the heart of Stellenbosch.
